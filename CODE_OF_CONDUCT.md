# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and harassment-free community.
Contributors should communicate constructively, assume good intent, accept
technical feedback, and focus criticism on code and behavior rather than people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks,
threats, publishing private information, and sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions
that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may result in a
temporary or permanent ban.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Reports will be
reviewed confidentially and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.
